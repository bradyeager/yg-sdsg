# SDSG Age-Group Mover Analysis

_From official San Diego Senior Games Fitness results, 2022-2025. Competition runs in 5-year age bands. For each competitor I track their band year-over-year; an observed band change pins their age, which projects their 2026 band. This flags only true **movers** for each Yeager's Gym athlete's 2026 division — competitors **rising in** from the band below, or **aged out** to the older band — who have **podiumed** in events we contest. Stable competitors already settled in the division are not movers and are not listed._

**Status:** `CLIMBING` = age math projects entry for 2026 · `WATCH` = multiple straight years in the band below (near the top, podium-caliber) · the goodbye tags (Sayonara, Buh-Bye, …) mark competitors who have **aged out** — no longer our problem.
